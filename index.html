About Us

The sparks foundation is an NGO working towards achieving the sustainable development goals through tree plantation. Just as the saying goes "A seed planted today, grows its branches tomorrow", we do believe that we must give back more than what we take from mother nature. We want to make it simple for anyone to save the environment by planting trees. Together we can restore forests, creat habitat for biodiversity, and make a positive social impact arount the world.
